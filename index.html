Rabbit Care Instructions

Daily Health Checklist

  • Check if the rabbit is eating and drinking.
  • Ensure the rabbit’s litter box is clean.
  • Inspect the rabbit’s fur for any signs of mats or parasites.
  • Check for any signs of distress or unusual behavior.
  • Ensure the rabbit has enough space to hop and stretch.
